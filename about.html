About us

Story About Masai Camp

Masai Camp has a history and tradition that stretches back more than 25 years as the first camp site in Arusha Tanzania. Over the years the Masai Camp brand has always been synonymous with elegance, quality and innovation: consisting of a large area – made up of parking space for Over-landers, Bikers and anyone on two or four wheels – allowing anybody with any equipment to get in a place with space and facilities to bring warmth, reliability, with outdoor activities, sports, crafts, and other . The ‘Camp’ allowed campers, locals and anyone who loved a unique and limited place with the same exquisite food, as well as an atmosphere of diversity in a single area. People would meet new people and old people would reunite with their friends and enjoy in a place found only in. Perhaps the most important part of Masai Camps’s history is the attention to little things that make it one of the most important places in Arusha, Hot showers, Laundry service, Free WiFi even an onsite mechanic are services offered in a way that the competition could not pick up. On top of this, Masai Camp still retains all the characteristic of a nice and safe campsite since 1993. Today Masai Camp offers the same traditional values and services and offers nothing less.

  • The guiding principle of the charity is to develop a sustainable way. The Arusha Children’s Trust actively and directly supports children in the Arusha and Rift Valley regions of Tanzania through enhancing education, improving health awareness, increasing knowledge of environmental conservation and upholding the traditions and culture of people within the region.
